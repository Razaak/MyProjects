FAQ's

What is the purpose of BBC Y?

The primary goal of the website is to get younger audiences more involved into what is going on. However, since everyone has their own specific interests, users will be allowed tp pick and choose what subjects they would like to subscribe to.

How do articles on BBC Y differ from those on other BBC websites?

Articles on BBC Y are wriiten by young adults for young adults. This can appear in the form of original articles or articles from the main BBC website that have been re-written in order to focus on how they relate to younger audiences.

Can anyone write for BBC Y?

Yes, anyone with enough expertise and experience in any of the BBC Y categories may become a contributor to the website. The website will also be hiring journalist who would like to intern with the website.

What is the relationship between the BBC and BBC Y?

BBC Y is a part of the BBC, however, it will be run and moderated by younger ineterns at the BBC who would like to gain experience at writing and researching and who understand better what younger audiences like.
